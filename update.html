The Great Suspender :: New Version is Out!

First things first. You may notice that any tabs you had suspended before the upgrade have now disappeared. Sorry about this! It's an unfortunate side-effect of how the tab suspension works. The good news is you can go to the history page and reload these missing tabs back into your browser.
There has been a lot of changes made since the last version. Most notably some bug fixes around the auto-suspend feature, and the whitelisting functionality.
- Introduced an option to never suspend pinned tabs. - New 1 minute and 5 minute options for time to auto-suspend. - Ability to choose image quality of screen preview. - Support chrome tab sync. Can now handle suspended tabs on another machine if it has the extension installed. - Can now prevent tabs with a half-completed form from suspending and losing your entered text.

Thanks again for using my extension. Any feedback / suggestions are always welcome. You can email me directly at deanoemcke@gmail.com or use chrome's various feedback mechanisms.
